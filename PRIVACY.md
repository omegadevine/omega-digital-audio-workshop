# Privacy Policy for Omega Digital Audio Workshop

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

## Overview

Omega Digital Audio Workshop ("Omega DAW", "we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

## Data Collection

Omega DAW does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information or user data to external servers.

### Local Data Storage

The application stores locally on your computer:
- Project files and audio recordings
- User preferences and settings
- Plugin configurations
- Session states

All data remains on your local machine and is never transmitted externally.

## Audio Processing

- All audio processing occurs locally on your device
- No audio data is sent to external servers
- Your recordings and projects are completely private

## Third-Party Plugins

If you use third-party VST/VST3 plugins, their privacy policies apply to their functionality. We are not responsible for third-party plugin behavior.

## Updates and Telemetry

- No usage analytics or telemetry are collected
- No crash reports are automatically sent
- Update checks (if implemented) only check version numbers

## Your Rights

You have complete control over all data created by Omega DAW since it resides on your local machine.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the repository with updated dates.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Open Source

This is an open-source project. You can review the source code to verify our privacy practices at:
